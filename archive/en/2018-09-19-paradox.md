(The following is based on an actual conversation I had with a friend)

Her: So, are the two of you a couple now?  
Me: What's a couple, really?... Look, it's the paradox of the heap: two people who have never went out on a date are not a couple, and a single date does not make a "non-couple" into a couple, then by induction this implies that couples cannot exist at all!
