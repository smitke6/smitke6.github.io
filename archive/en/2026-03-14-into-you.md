### Is she into you? The definitive guide

| If she... | Then she... |
| :--- | :--- |
| acts aloof and indifferent | is not into you |
| seems to sort of like you | hasn't decided yet (don’t worry, pretty soon she’ll decide she doesn’t like you) |
| talks to you every day, laughs at your jokes, shows interest in everything you do, and wants to hang out often (preferably alone) | likes you as a friend |
| calls you names, tries to insult and belittle you | likes you **way too much** |
| compliments you on your looks | is probably married |
| smiles every time she sees you, her pupils are dilated when you talk to her, and she acts awkward whenever you’re around (she giggles, blushes, stutters, etc.) | **definitely** has a boyfriend |
| asks for your phone number | has a girlfriend |
